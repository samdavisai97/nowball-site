Delete Account

How to permanently delete your NowBall account and all associated data.

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data — including your profile, game history, stats, and ratings — will be permanently removed.

How to Delete Your Account

  • 1 Open the NowBall app on your device.
  • 2 Tap your Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
  • 3 Tap the Settings icon in the top corner.
  • 4 Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  • 5 Confirm the deletion when prompted. Your account will be permanently deleted immediately.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • ✓Your profile (name, photo, bio, positions)
  • ✓Your game history and statistics
  • ✓Your ratings and MVP records
  • ✓Your login credentials
